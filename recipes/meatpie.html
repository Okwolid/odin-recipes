meatpie

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter or margarine
  • salt
  • cold water
  • minced meat
  • onion
  • carrot
  • potato
  • seasoning cube
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and butter until it forms a crumbly texture.
  2. Add cold water gradually to form a smooth dough, then refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, add minced meat, onions, carrot, and potatoes.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and seasoning cubes; cook until tender.
  5. Roll out the dough and cut into round shapes using a cutter or bowl.
  6. Place filling in the center of each round and fold over to form a semi-circle.
  7. Press edges with a fork to seal.
  8. Brush with egg wash for golden color.
  9. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes or until golden brown.

This Nigerian meat pie is crispy outside, soft inside, and filled with a savory, juicy meat and veggie mix.

Best served warm with tea, soft drinks, or as a snack on the go!
