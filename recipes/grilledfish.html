grilledfish

Ingredients

  • fresh fish (tilapia, croaker, or mackerel)
  • vegetable oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onions
  • lemon juice
  • seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Clean and wash the fish thoroughly, removing scales and guts.
  2. Pat dry and make diagonal cuts on both sides for even marination.
  3. In a bowl, mix oil, pepper, garlic, ginger, onions, seasoning, and lemon juice.
  4. Rub the marinade generously inside and outside the fish.
  5. Let it sit for 30 minutes to 1 hour to absorb the flavors.
  6. Preheat grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  7. Grill each side for 7–10 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Brush with leftover marinade while grilling for extra flavor.
  9. Serve hot with fried plantain, salad, or chips.

This Nigerian-style grilled fish is spicy, smoky, and packed with flavor — perfect for a weekend hangout or dinner.

Best enjoyed hot with a cold drink or fresh salad!
